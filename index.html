My Design Process

my design process is creating a product journey where both the user's wants and needs for functionality are perfectly balanced through both broad exploration and focused execution.

1

Explore

My initial goal is to gather as much information and resources about the project as possible. I then use this information to reach out to people to begin working together to create clear expectations for the end product. In addition, I will document any current processes that may contribute to creating the new product.

2

Generate Ideas

I create quick and simple sketches of ideas and wireframes of potential user interfaces. I experiment with interested designs to discover which designs will best match the needs of the user. During this stage, my focus is on learning rather than creating the best product.

3

Design

When I have established a direction for the project, I design a usable interface by following the established visual hierarchy, ensuring accessibility for all users, and maintaining consistency between various screens within the design. If an element looks great but has a negative effect on usability, I will make adjustments.

4

Evaluate

After I have completed the design, I will have it reviewed and tested to ensure usability by others besides myself. I will perform multiple iterations of the design based on user testing results so that the final product matches what the end user requires.
